Welcome to NUV

Welcome to Navrachana University, Vadodara (NUV), where education unlocks potential, sparks change, and shapes a brighter future. Our mission transcends traditional learning; we empower students to become transformative leaders, ready to thrive in an ever-evolving world. Central to our philosophy is the belief: "Empowering minds through experiential education."

Located in the vibrant city of Vadodara, NUV offers an interdisciplinary approach that combines academic excellence with hands-on experience. Our future-focused curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, critical thinking, and creativity, equipping students to face real-world challenges with confidence.

Schools of Navrachana

School of Liberal Studies and Education: BA-Journalism & Mass Communication, BA-Humanities and Social Sciences, BEd, PhD.

School of Science: BSc (Chemistry, Life Sciences, Microbiology), BSc-MSc Integrated (Biomedical), MSc (Chemistry, Microbiology, Life Science, Clinical Embryology).

School of Environmental Design and Architecture: Bachelor of Architecture, Bachelor of Design (Interior, Product Design, Visual Communication).

School of Engineering and Technology: BCA, BSc (Data Science), BTech (Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Civil, CSE), MTech.

School of Business and Law: BBA, BBA-LLB, LLM, MBA, Executive MBA.

All programs follow modular, semester-wise curricula and allow cross-disciplinary learning. The unitary structure promotes innovation, interdisciplinary thinking, and a holistic academic and life experience.
